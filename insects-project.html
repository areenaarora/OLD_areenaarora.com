The world needs more food.


-Are insects the way to go?

In 2000, the world had 800 million undernourished people, according to data from UN's Food and Agriculture Organization. Undernourished population is defined as an estimate of the population whose food consumption "is insufficient to provide the dietary energy levels required to maintain a normal active and healthy life".
By 2020, the latest available data, the number of undernourished people fell to 768 million, jumping significantly from 2019.
